Timing Services

High school track and field timing centers around accuracy and precision. RED Timing's experienced timing crew utilizes the latest FinishLynx fully automatic timing system to deliver results quickly and accurately.


We pride ourselves on rapid turnarounds to keep your meet moving along. Handheld stopwatches and plunger timers are outdated and add a larger margin for error. Fully automatic timing systems operated by RED Timing are the best way to run a track meet of any size.


RED Timing operates in southeast Wisconsin. For the past several years, the RED Timing crew has run many of the dual, quad, invitational, and conference meets in the Rock Valley Conference.
